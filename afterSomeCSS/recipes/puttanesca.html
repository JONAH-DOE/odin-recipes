Puttanesca Recipe

What is puttanesca?

Puttanesca sauce has a robust flavor. Itâ€™s a bold and savory Italian sauce made with tomatoes, anchovies, capers, garlic, and olives, perfect for enhancing pasta dishes.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Capers
  • Anchovies
  • Olives
  • Pasta

Steps

  1. Cook garlic and red pepper flakes in oil. Add capers and anchovies and cook.
  2. Squeeze whole tomatoes into the pan (discard the juices).
  3. Add crushed tomatoes and olives and simmer (recipe below).
  4. Season with salt and pepper and serve as desired.
