Puttanesca Recipe

What is puttanesca?

Puttanesca sauce has a robust flavor. Itâ€™s a bold and savory Italian sauce made with tomatoes, anchovies, capers, garlic, and olives, perfect for enhancing pasta dishes.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Capers
  • Anchovies
  • Olives
  • Pasta

Steps

  • Cook garlic and red pepper flakes in oil. Add capers and anchovies and cook.
  • Squeeze whole tomatoes into the pan (discard the juices).
  • Add crushed tomatoes and olives and simmer (recipe below).
  • Season with salt and pepper and serve as desired.
